The pressure you're under

Nordic fintech is entering a margin compression era. CMOs are being held accountable for growth while being cut out of product conversations. Embedded finance is spreading distribution. Who owns the customer is getting more vague — and testing is harder when the season is moving your baseline.

This academy exists to give you one fewer unknown. Concrete frameworks, real data, and thinking built for B2B executives navigating this exact moment.
