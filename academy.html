About the Academy

Hi, I’m Alexandra Grisanti, based in Stockholm. I work with design systems, brand structure, and performance-driven creative work every day — helping teams build clarity, speed, and trust into their work. I believe knowledge compounds when it’s shared. Teaching and learning are part of the same process. It's rewarding to breaking down complex ideas into practical frameworks that others can use.
