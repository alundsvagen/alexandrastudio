Why this exists

Digital channels are exhausted.
People need authenticity.

Clicks don't convert the way they used to. Especially in fintech where we know, Trust is everything. AI is accelerating content noise, and the fintechs that win in the next cycle are the ones building real relationships — in person, in context, with the right people at the table.

My work is structured around a simple belief: that a six-month program of deliberate conversations, curated introductions, and iterated pilots produces more durable traction than digital campaigns.

The 6-month arc
Month 1 – 2
Conversations & introductions
The right meetings happen in the right rooms. We build the access — events, dinners, agendas where they want to meet you.
Month 2 – 4
Pilots & early traction
Conversations turn into pilots. Product design and onboarding begins with the first real clients. Be hands on and ready to iterate with real customers. Feedback is priceless.
Month 4 – 5
Testing & iteration
We iterate fast on what works — product, positioning, and process refined with live feedback. Build this back into your branding and messaging to compound.
Month 6
Solid, stable traction
A repeatable growth motion. Real clients, a refined product, and a community that knows who you are.
